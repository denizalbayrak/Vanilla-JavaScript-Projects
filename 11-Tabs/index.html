about

Vegas isn’t just a cat—he’s the king of comfort, master of mischief, and the undisputed ruler of the living room. Whether he's lounging like royalty, knocking things off shelves with purpose, or giving judgmental stares from across the room, he carries himself with flair. Life with Vegas is anything but boring.

history

He strutted into our lives like he already owned the place—and honestly, he wasn’t wrong. What started as cautious steps and curious sniffs soon turned into full-blown sofa domination. Over time, he’s gone from a playful kitten to a wise, fluffy force of nature who knows exactly where the snacks are hidden.

vision

Vegas envisions a world where every nap is sacred, every sunbeam is reserved seating, and every human instinctively knows when it’s treat time. He believes in the power of calm stares, strategic purring, and the occasional chaos to keep life interesting. His dream? A home (and world) ruled by comfort, curiosity, and catnip.

goals

  • -Wake up the humans just before their alarm—because timing is everything.
  • -Claim every soft surface in the house as his personal throne.
  • -Master the art of the dramatic stare to get snacks on demand.
  • -Establish a daily cuddle quota and enforce it strictly.
  • -Become the face of global “Catfluence” and inspire others to live like Vegas: confidently and cozily.
